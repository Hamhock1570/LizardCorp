Codefest Survival Guide

Tips:

  1. Make sure your team is on the same page. Work as a group. It's okay to be a leader, but don't bulldoze your teamates.
  2. Design before you code. Spend your first night designing your datas models and planning how you Application/ Site is going to be used.
  3. Setup up your source control before you start coding. Github is your friend.
  4. Break apart your project into small, managable tasks and assign them out to your group. Try Trello, or just draw them out on a whiteboard.
  5. Make sure your goal is realistic for the amount of time you have. Don't bite off more than you can chew.
  6. Stick to what you know. It's a good time to learn some new skills, but you still need to deliver a product. If you nobody has any idea about web development, but you know Swing/JavaFX... make a desktop App.
  7. If you are stuck on something for longer than 30 minutes. ASK FOR HELP!!!!! info@LizardCorp.com
  8. If you find that your goals are unrealistic, feel free to cut back the scope. However, be very cautious before expanding the scope of your project.
  9. Focus on functionality over aesthetics. Build out your features and make your idea come to life.
  10. Have fun.
